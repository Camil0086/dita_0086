Solid wood furniture stands for environmental friendliness and sustainability

If you want to buy new furniture and at the same time want to do something good for the environment, you should rely on solid wood. This results not only in advantages for nature but also for the individual.

Solid wood furniture removes carbon dioxide from the atmosphere.

Not only that solid wood furniture looks noble, is of high quality and has a long lifespan, it also helps the ecosystem to bind the harmful greenhouse gas CO2 in its own wood for a long time and thus manage to counteract climate change.

But what exactly does it mean?

Of course, there is a somewhat difficult mechanism behind this, but also not completely unknown, as we will now explain to you in detail:

CO2 smog as the main cause of climate change.

Keyword photosynthesis:

One or the other has probably heard of this term in biology class. But how was that again exactly? "Something to say that carbon dioxide in connection with solar energy creates products like sugar and oxygen, right?" Roughly summarized, you could describe it like this.

Photosynthesis describes more precisely how green plants produce energy-rich substances (glucose C6H12O6 and oxygen O2) from low-energy substances (carbon dioxide CO2 and water H2O) using solar energy. So one can say that plants are something like small “solar power plants” that enable us to live on earth through their end products.

And now comes the crucial thing that made us take up the topic in our blog. The trees / forests bind the carbon dioxide from the air, which has been converted by photosynthesis, into their own wood, and that permanently! This in turn has a positive effect on the environment and can also be decisive for the ecological balance.

Sunlight stimulates plants to photosynthesize

Why solid wood furniture?

By manufacturing furniture from solid wood, as we have found, carbon dioxide is permanently removed from the cycle of nature. What does it mean?

  • positive impact on the greenhouse effect
  • improvement of the atmosphere, thus more air to breathe
  • counteracting climate change
  • stabilization of the ecological budget

So if you want to buy furniture in the near future and want to do something good for the environment, you should rely on solid wood.
